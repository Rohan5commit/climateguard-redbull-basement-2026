# ClimateGuard Problem Story

## Emotional Problem Narrative

A flood warning pops up at 11:42 PM. You check three apps, each with different maps, terms, and confidence levels. Your parent asks, "Should we leave now or wait?" You still do not know if your exact block, your route out, or your basement are at risk. The cost of uncertainty is panic, delayed action, and preventable loss.

ClimateGuard exists for that moment: one address in, one clear answer out.
